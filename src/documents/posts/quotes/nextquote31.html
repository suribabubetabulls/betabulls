Quotes of the Week

Posted by Admin | July 31, 2015

Lots of companies don't succeed over time. What do they fundamentally do wrong? They usually miss the future.

– Larry Page, Google

Knowledge is power. Knowledge shared is power multiplied.

– Robert Noyce, Intel

Everybody wants to get enlightened but nobody wants to change.

– Andrew Cohen, Qualcomm

You have to always look ahead, always be positive. It's your choice, your destiny. You have to enjoy life.

- Stan Shih, Acer

Creativity comes from looking for the unexpected and stepping outside your own experience.

– Masaru Ibuka, Sony

Don't be afraid to make a mistake. But make sure you don't make the same mistake twice.

– Akio Morita, Sony

The greatest sign of success for a teacher... is to be able to say, 'The children are now working as if I did not exist.'

– Maria Montessori, Psychologists

Hope is a necessity for normal life and the major weapon against the suicide impulse.

– Karl A. Menninger, Psychiatrist

The way you 'take history' is also a way of 'making history.'

– Erik Erikson, Psychologists

Good health is essential to happiness, and happiness is an essential to good citizenship. 

– Charles Mayo,  Pediatricians
